Privacy Policy

App: A-BEE-ary · Last updated: 25 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile game A-BEE-ary (the “App”), developed by Andrejs Mankevics (the “Developer”, “we”, “us”), handles information when you use it. By installing or using the App you agree to this policy.

1. Who we are

The App is an offline, single-player idle game. There are no user accounts, no login, and no social features. The Developer is an individual based in Latvia (European Union). Contact: a.mankevics@gmail.com.

2. Information we collect ourselves

None. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to our own servers. We have no servers. Your game progress (coins, hives, settings) is saved only locally on your device and is removed when you uninstall the App.

3. Information collected by advertising (Google AdMob)

The App displays ads through Google AdMob, a service provided by Google. To serve and measure ads, the Google Mobile Ads SDK and Google’s advertising partners may collect and process certain information from your device, including:

  • your device’s advertising identifier (e.g. Android Advertising ID);
  • IP address and approximate (non-precise) location derived from it;
  • device information (model, operating system, language, app version);
  • ad interaction and usage data (ads shown, taps), used for delivery, measurement and fraud prevention.

This processing is carried out by Google as a third party. We do not receive this data in a form that identifies you. For details on how Google uses this information, see Google’s Privacy Policy and How Google uses information from sites or apps that use its services.

4. Consent for users in the EEA, UK and Switzerland

If you are located in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom or Switzerland, the App shows a consent message (via Google’s User Messaging Platform) before personalized ads are served, in accordance with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). You can grant or decline consent when the message is shown. If you decline, you will see only non-personalized ads. You can also withdraw consent later by resetting your advertising identifier in your device settings (see section 6) or by reinstalling the App.

5. Legal basis (GDPR)

Where the GDPR applies, our legal basis for the processing described above is your consent (for personalized advertising) and our and Google’s legitimate interests in showing non-personalized ads, ad measurement and preventing fraud.

6. Your rights

You can:

  • reset or delete your advertising identifier in your device settings (Settings → Google → Ads);
  • opt out of ad personalization in your device settings (Settings → Google → Ads);
  • under the GDPR, request access to, correction or erasure of your personal data, and lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. Because we hold no personal data ourselves, requests about ad data should also be directed to Google.

7. Children

The App is intended for a general audience aged 13 and over. It is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

8. Data retention and security

We retain no personal data. Local game-save data stays on your device under your control. Data processed by Google for advertising is retained according to Google’s own policies. While no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, we rely on Google’s industry-standard safeguards for ad-related data.
